2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following personal and business data when you submit our waitlist form:

Business Email Address Personal Data To contact you about your submission, validation results, and early access invitations.

We do not collect:

  • Payment card details or financial information (no payment is taken at sign-up).

3. How We Collect Data

We collect data through the following means:

  • Waitlist form submissions — when you fill in and submit the form on our website.
  • Email communications — when you reply to or interact with our emails.
  • Cookies and analytics — we may use basic analytics tools to understand how visitors use our site (see Section 8 on Cookies).

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases for processing your personal data:

  • Consent — by submitting the waitlist form, you consent to us contacting you about your submission and relevant product updates. You may withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Legitimate Interests — we have a legitimate business interest in analysing aggregated pain point data to determine which software products to build. This processing does not override your rights or freedoms.
  • Contract — if you enter into a pre-order or purchase agreement with us in the future, we will process your data as necessary to fulfil that contract.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide for the following purposes:

  • To acknowledge your waitlist submission and keep you informed of your status.
  • To validate demand by analysing patterns across submissions (in aggregated, anonymised form where possible).
  • To send you an early bird pre-order invitation if your pain point is validated.
  • To communicate product updates, launch announcements, and founding member information.
  • To improve our website and submission process.

We will never:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties.
  • Use your data for unrelated marketing by third parties.
  • Share your individually identifiable submission data publicly.

6. Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your waitlist submission data is stored in Supabase, a third-party database provider. Supabase stores data in secure, encrypted cloud infrastructure. Data may be stored in servers located within the European Economic Area (EEA) or in other regions with appropriate data transfer safeguards.

Security Measures

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website.
  • Restricted access to your submission data — only authorised team members can view it.
  • Use of reputable, security-certified third-party infrastructure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, or until you request deletion. If you do not become a paying customer within 24 months of submitting the waitlist form, we will review and, where appropriate, delete your data.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a data subject under UK GDPR, you have the following rights:

👁 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

✏️ Right to Rectification

You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.

🗑 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

⏸ Right to Restriction

You can ask us to restrict how we process your data in certain circumstances.

📦 Right to Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

🚫 Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at buzinesspainkiller@outlook.com. We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have not handled your data appropriately.
